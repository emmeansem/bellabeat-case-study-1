# Bellabeat Case Study: How Can a Wellness Technology Company Play It Smart?

## 📌 Business Task

Analyze smart device usage data to identify trends and apply those insights to Bellabeat's product marketing strategy.

## 🏢 About Bellabeat

Bellabeat is a high-tech company manufacturing wellness-focused smart products for women, such as the Leaf tracker, Time watch, Spring bottle, and the Bellabeat app.

## 🔍 Data Sources

- **Primary**: [Fitbit Fitness Tracker Data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/arashnic/fitbit)
- **Limitations**: Small sample, user bias, no Bellabeat-specific data

## 🛠 Data Preparation & Processing

- Used R and spreadsheets for cleaning
- Removed NA values and duplicates
- Joined activity and sleep datasets for combined insights

## 📊 Analysis Summary

- Users average 7,500 steps/day; only 20% meet 10,000-step goal
- Most users are less active on weekends
- Sleep averages are below recommended levels

## 🔑 Key Insights

- Target audience often doesn't reach activity or sleep goals
- Bellabeat can promote app features to encourage goal tracking and consistency
- Mindfulness features underused; opportunity to educate and promote

## ✅ Recommendations

1. Highlight sleep and activity tracking in marketing
2. Use personalized goal nudges
3. Develop weekend engagement strategies
4. Promote premium mindfulness features

## 🚀 Final Thoughts

Bellabeat is positioned to grow by aligning its product capabilities with wellness trends. Data-driven marketing and user-friendly app features will help drive engagement and improve user outcomes.
